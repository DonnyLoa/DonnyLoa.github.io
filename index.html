Bear Creek High School - Stockton, California

It was during my junior and senior years at Bear Creek High School that I first delved into computer science and dedicated my time towards learning C++. There, I adopted the logical mindset and basic programming practices required in designing and developing programs, including how to collaborate with my fellow peers, think in terms of code syntax, and draft my projects and ideas before development. Google CSSI - Kirkland, Washington

During the summer of 2018, I attended the Google Computer Science Summer Institute program, where I greatly broadened my knowledge and expanded my experience with computer programming. Included in my time there, I learned how to program in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python, incorporate Google App Engine in setting up and deploying an application, and utilize GitHub for collaborative projects. For our final week, I collaborated with two other peers to design, develop, and present the web app game Magic Trivia to Google engineers, showcasing and incorporating all we had learned during our time at the program.
